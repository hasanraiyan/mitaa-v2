Our Foundation

Dedicated to MIT and its Alumni

The MIT Alumni Association (MITAA) serves as the bridge between Muzaffarpur Institute of Technology and its esteemed graduates worldwide. Our mission is to foster a spirit of loyalty and fraternity, support the institute's goals, and provide avenues for professional and personal growth for all members.
